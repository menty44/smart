Metro Kenya youth and children’s Operations is a non-profit organization based in Kenya. We aim to empower the youth and children through education and access of information, youth empowerment and talent promotion, creation of opportunity for youth , community development and youth participation, environment and youth leadership, street outreach, rehabilitation of street children reintegrating children back to communities sensitization and advocacy to promote and protect children’s rights.

Metro Kenya Youth and Children Operation

Background Info

Metro Kenya Youth and Children Operation is an organization that mainly works with youth and children, specifically Street Children. It helps them to realize their skills and abilities. Also, it rehabilitates street children from the streets.

Our Services

Developing structures and formulating policies, that is sustainable to establish effective and efficient methods of rehabilitating and reforming the youth and street children
To have a productive society free of street families, drugs and sexually transmitted diseases.

Our Objectives

We want to make a difference in the lives of the street children and show them that we appreciate them and that they are a part of the community too, and also to teach them on the awareness on drugs, unwanted pregnancies, crime and anything that would jeopardize their achievements in life.

Civic Education

By providing this we ensure that they maintain good behaviour in society and respect the law of the land by stipulating clearly the consequences that follow failiure to do so.

Rehabilitation of street children

We take great pride getting kids out of the streets where they waste their lives away and guiding them towards the right path that will lead them to success.

Identifying Youth talent

We gather the youth in different locations and search for talent amongst them and help develop them and mentor them in making a life for themselves through exloiting their abilities.
